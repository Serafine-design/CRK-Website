Black Raisin Cookie

No one can hide from the keen Black Raisin Eye! The Cookie vanishes into the shadows, appears amidst the enemy ranks (targets Cookies first), and strikes several times, dealing substantial area damage. (Magic Candy) Upon using her skill, Black Raisin Cookie will deal more area damage hits and amplify debuffs received by the enemies. Additionally, while the skill is active, Black Raisin Cookie's CRIT DMG will increase, and she will deal greater area damage after scoring a critical hit.

  • Single-hit DMG: 79.7%
  • Consuming Darkness: CRIT% -5.0%, Healing -5.0% for 8.0 sec; stacks up to x3
  • Area DMG Hits: +3 times; 8 times in total
  • Area DMG After CRIT: 195.0% of ATK
  • Skill CRIT DMG: +170.0
  • Amplified Debuffs: +5.0% for 10 sec; stacks up to x1
