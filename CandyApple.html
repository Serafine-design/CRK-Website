Candy Apple Cookie

Candy Apple Cookie slams down a giant lollipop with a heave. Enemies hit will receive the ATK Reduction and DMG Amplification debuffs. The lollipop will shatter, granting HP Shields for all allies, and candy fragments will deal DMG to enemies and inflict the DMG Amplification debuff.

  • DMG: 668.1% of ATK
  • ATK: -30.0% for 12 sec; stacks up to x1
  • DMG: +47.5% for 12 sec; stacks up to x1
  • DEF: -40.0% for 12 sec; stacks up to x1
  • DMG: 329.6% of ATK
  • Team HP Shield: 30.0% of Max HP for 12 sec; stacks up to x1
  • Team ATK: +15.0% for 12 sec; stacks up to x1
  • DMG: +47.5% for 12 sec; stacks up to x1

Cookie Setting

Wanted Stats

  • Crit %
  • Attack
  • Cooldown
  • DMG resist

In Game Info

  • Used only in Dragon guild raid with Black Sapphire and Shadow milk for the Deceitful Trio passive. Therefore if you don't have all 3 cookies, she is useless.
