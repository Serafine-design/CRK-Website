Pudding à la Mode Cookie

Pudding à la Mode Cookie's arms transform into plasma cannons and emit powerful plasma beams towards enemies. Next, the Cookie will call upon her drones, which attack the same targets during her regular attacks. Drone attacks are regarded as Pudding à la Mode Cookie's regular attacks. Drones will prioritize targets with a Penumbral Mark. The Team Drizzle effect will activate when entering battle with Choco Drizzle Cookie and Green Tea Mousse Cookie.

  • Plasma Cannon DMG: x30 hits over 1.2 sec; 671.9% Total DMG
  • CRIT%: +10.0% for 15 sec; stacks up to x1
  • ATK: +12.5% for 15 sec; stacks up to x1
  • Zap: 2.8% every 0.5 sec for 5 sec; stacks up to x1, nullifies HP Shield
  • Drones: x3 for 13 sec
  • Pudding à la Mode Cookie's ATK SPD: +27.0% while Drones are active
  • Drone Single-hit DMG: 18.6% of ATK; drones prioritize targets inflicted with Penumbral Mark
Team Drizzle
  • Plasma Cannon Extra DMG (Cookies): 0.4% of Max HP per hit
  • Drone Single-hit Extra DMG: 11.0% of ATK
  • Drone DEF Reduction: -4.5% for 10 sec; stacks up to x12
