Wedding Cake Cookie

Turns the battlefield into a luxurious Wedding Aisle. The guests—your team—receive Healing while the enemies sustain Periodic Damage. Summoned Creatures become Stunned. Additionally, Wedding Cake Cookie will increase the team's ATK and CRIT DMG. Wedding Fireworks deal Additional DMG to enemies and Stun Summoned Creatures. If Black Forest Cookie is on the team, she will gain the Star of the Hour buff.

  • Wedding Aisle DMG: 96.8% of ATK every 0.5 sec for 3 sec
  • Wedding Aisle Stun: 1 sec
  • Wedding Fireworks DMG: 145.2% every 0.25 sec for 1.5 sec
  • Wedding Fireworks Stun: 1 sec
  • Healing to Allies: 122.5% of ATK
  • ATK: +5.0% for 10 sec; stacks up to x1
  • CRIT DMG: +17.0% for 10 sec; stacks up to x1
Star of the Hour
  • Cooldown: -10.0%
  • Wedding Aisle Additional DMG to Summoned Creatures: 352.5%
  • Applies Star of the Wedding buff to Transformed Black Forest Cookie: +20.0% ATK SPD, +80.0% CRIT DMG for 15 sec
