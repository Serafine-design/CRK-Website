Blueberry Pie Cookie

An evil spirit appears from the Tome, dealing damage to the enemies and inflicting them with the Greed of the Tome debuff. It will also heal Blueberry Pie Cookieâ€™s HP and increase her DMG Resist. The Tome will gain a stack of Greed whenever the spirit deals damage to Cookies, or the target inflicted with the Greed of the Tome debuff becomes incapacitated. Once the Tome has enough stacks of Greed, it will unleash its Sealed Power, increasing Blueberry Pie Cookie's ATK and dealing DMG to the enemies. While Blueberry Pie Cookie is using her skill, she will briefly become resistant to interrupting effects. DMG proportional to Max HP is capped at 300,000. (Magic Candy) Blueberry Pie Cookie becomes more powerful with the hidden secrets of the tome. She deals extra damage with Sealed Power and inflicts hit enemies with Zap. She also applies the Current Charge buff to Electricity-type Cookies upon using her skill.

  • Greed: Sealed Power triggers upon x7 stacks
  • Sealed Power DMG (Cookies): up to x6 hits over 1.5 sec, 87.3% DMG + True DMG equal to 0.95% of HP per hit
  • Poison-type DMG (Allies): +35.0% as long as Black Sapphire Cookie in present on the battlefield
  • Sealed Power DMG (Others): up to x6 hits over 1.5 sec, 17.7% DMG per hit
  • ATK: +25.0% for 3.0 sec
