Twizzly Gummy Cookie

Twizzly Gummy Cookie overloads her Electrojelly Gun and fires a powerful electrifying laser beam. While Twizzly Gummy Cookie is using her skill, her CRIT DMG is increased and enemies hit by her laser beam will be Zapped. This is a non-stackable debuff that deals periodic damage and temporarily disrupts HP Shields. While Twizzly Gummy Cookie is charging her laser, she will briefly become resistant to interrupting effects. (Magic Candy) Applies the Current Charge buff to Electricity-type Cookies upon using her skill. Her Electrojelly Gun from the future deals extra damage, and stacks Zap debuffs. Twizzly Gummy Cookie's Skill CRIT DMG buff becomes even stronger while she is using her skill.

  • Single-hit DMG (Cookies): 19.4%
  • Single-hit DMG (Others): 11.6%
  • Skill CRIT DMG: +350.0%
  • Zap:2.8% DMG every 1.0 sec for 7.0 sec. Nullifies HP Shield.
Magic Candy
  • Laser Beam Extra DMG: 40.0% of ATK
  • Skill CRIT DMG: changed to +400.0%
  • Zap: changed to stacks up to x5
  • Current Charge: CRIT% +7.5% for 15 sec; stacks up to x2
  • CRIT DMG: +20.0% for 15 sec; stacks up to x1
