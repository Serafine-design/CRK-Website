Black Forest Cookie

Black Forest Cookie uses the power granted by the Witches to Transform. In this form, Black Forest Cookie gains increased ATK SPD and DMG Resist, and Immunity. Also, she will deal Area Damage with her Regular Attacks. If the target is a Summoned Creature, Black Forest Cookie will deal Additional Damage and Stun them with a certain chance. At the end of her transformation, she will summon Thorns to deal wide-range Area Damage and Stun her enemies. If Wedding Cake Cookie is on the team, she will gain the Star of the Hour buff.

  • Transformation: 8 sec
  • Regular Attack DMG During Transformation: 421.5% of ATK + Additional DMG to Summoned Creatures 200.7%
  • Stun to Summoned Creatures: 1 sec with 50.0% chance
  • Thorns DMG: 1058.7% of ATK + Additional DMG to Summoned Creatures 1,048.1%
  • Final Hit Stun: 2 secs
  • ATK SPD: +15.0% during Transformation
  • Resist: +25.0% during Transformation
  • lasts during Transformation
Star of the Hour
  • Cooldown: -10.0%
  • Amplifies Transformation buffs:
  • ATK SPD: +30.0% on top of the regular ATK SPD buff during Transformation
  • Stun to Summoned Creatures: 100% chance
  • Additional DMG to Summoned Creatures: 941.6%
