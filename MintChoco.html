Mint Choco Cookie

Mint Choco Cookie plays a sublime melody that increases the team's ATK SPD, applies the Warm Breeze buff, and grants HP Shield to Wind-type Cookies. Upon playing a melodic tune, he inflicts damage on enemies with his Mint Gust and heals allies' HP equal to a portion of the damage inflicted. Once Mint Choco Cookie gains enough Tailwind stacks, he will heal his allies once more with a refreshing performance. (Magic Candy) Mint Choco Cookie gains the Tailwind buff at the beginning of a battle. Upon using his skill, he will increase Wind-type Cookies' ATK and heal the allies according to the number of his Tailwind stacks. Mint Choco Cookie's Mint Gust changes to Mint Cyclone which will send enemies Flying. Flying Targets will receive increased Wind-type damage, and their ATK will decrease.

  • Healing: 143.1% of ATK
  • Mint Gust DMG: 913.8%
  • Mint Gust Healing: 13.5% of Mist Gust DMG: capped at 150.0% of current ATK
  • ATK SPD: +40.0% for 10.0 sec; stacks up to x2
  • Warm Breeze: DMG Resist +15.0%, ATK SPD Debuff Resist +15.0% for 10.0 sec; stacks up to x2
  • HP Shield: blocks 10.0% of Max HP for 5.0 sec
  • Bonus Healing: 11.3% of team's ATK when x1 stacks of Tailwind are reached
  • Tailwind: DMG Resist +10.0%, ATK SPD +7.0% (self) for 10.0 sec upon regular attacks; stacks up to x3
Magic Candy
  • Mint Cyclone DMG: 450.8~913.8%
  • Mint Cyclone Healing: 13.5% of Mint Cyclone DMG; capped at 150.0% of current ATK
  • ATK: +12.5% for 10 sec; stacks up to x3
  • Extra Healing: 17.5% per stack of Tailwind
  • ATK: -15.0% for 10 sec; stacks up to x1
